To promote greater lighting efficiency and smarter energy use, the U.S. Congress passed energy efficiency legislation with numerous benefits for the economy and the public.

New efficiency standards will place lumens per watt regulations on linear and U-shaped fluorescent lamps on July 14th.
Due to the new standards and regulations, GE will begin to phase out standard SP T8 lamps next month.
